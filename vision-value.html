Code of Ethics

We live by a set of values and adhere to a code of ethics that guide us every single day.

Our Values

  • 👤
    Integrity:
    We act ethically and transparently, prioritizing doing the right thing over business interests. We do the right thing even when no one is watching.
  • 💡
    Innovation:
    We are in a constant state of inquiry constantly exploring how to make our training even better. We are curious and open to new possibilities.
  • 🙏
    Humility:
    There is no innovation without humility. We acknowledge that we know very little and that our perspective and point of view is just one in many. We honour every story equally and learn from each and every being and situation that crosses our path.
  • 😇
    Honesty:
    We are transparent and honest with information and processes and in our relationships with others. We create a culture where truth is at the forefront, where everyone can express their opinion openly without fear.
  • 🌍
    Respect:
    Respect for all living beings, humans and beings of the More-than-Human World. This is at the core of Relational Forest Therapy, where we respect nature as kin, instead of solely using her for our personal Benefit and wellbeing.
  • 🔄
    Reciprocity:
    We believe in a culture of healthy relationships with humans and More-than-Humans. Every action that we take is followed by the question, how can we give back?
  • ❤️
    Kindness:
    Every being deserves being treated in a kind way. Everyone is living their lives the best they can, we know very little about their struggles. One single Word of Kindness can change a life.
  • 🤝
    Compassion:
    When we know about someone else's situation, we try to put ourselves in their shoes to actively understand and empathize without judgment or assumptions.
  • 🔥
    Passion:
    We believe in what we do with all of our being, heart and soul. We live for what we do. We believe that there is a powerful ripple effect going into the world when we do things with passion.
  • ⭐
    Excellence:
    We take what we do very seriously. We review our curriculum and pedagogy constantly, we receive feedback with gratitude to improve every day.
